Privacy Policy

GreenPath collects limited personal information such as your Instagram public profile, journey data, and uploaded photos/videos to provide eco-route recommendations, gamification features, and eco-impact tracking. We also collect technical information for analytics and app improvement.

Data Collection

  • Instagram profile information: username, profile picture, and public profile details
  • Journey data: source, destination, routes chosen, travel mode, CO₂ emissions, points, badges, and eco-tasks
  • Photos and videos you upload (with date/time metadata)
  • Technical information: device/browser type, IP address, usage statistics

How We Use Your Data

  • Personalize your experience and recommendations
  • Calculate and display your eco-impact and gamification progress
  • Enable social sharing features (with your permission)
  • Improve GreenPath through analytics (aggregated/anonymized)

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties except as needed for GreenPath’s core functionality or if required by law.
  • Social sharing is only done with your explicit consent.

Data Security

  • Your data is stored securely and encrypted.
  • Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel only.

Your Rights

  • Contact us at info@yourdomain.com to request deletion of your data or your account.
  • You can edit your profile and delete uploaded content at any time.

Children’s Privacy

GreenPath is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Policy Updates

Major changes to this policy will be communicated via email or in-app notification.
